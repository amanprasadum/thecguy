Types of External Sealing

We offer a variety of external sealing options to meet your needs.

Silicone Sealing

Ideal for flexible joints and areas exposed to high movement.

Polyurethane Sealing

Provides strong adhesion and is perfect for areas requiring durable, weather-resistant seals.

Acrylic Sealing

Suitable for areas that need to be painted over, offering a clean finish.

Butyl Rubber Sealing

Great for sealing joints in roofing and guttering systems due to its excellent waterproofing properties.
