Our Services

External Sealing Services & Types

Our external sealing services are designed to provide comprehensive protection for your property. We offer a variety of external sealing options to meet your needs:

  • Facade Sealing: Complete facade protection using advanced sealing techniques for weather resistance.
  • Window and Door Sealing: Comprehensive sealing solutions to prevent drafts and water infiltration.
  • Expansion Joint Sealing: Professional sealing to accommodate structural movement and prevent damage.
  • Roof and Gutter Sealing: Specialized sealing services for roofing systems and guttering protection.

Sealing Materials & Maintenance

We use various high-quality sealing materials and provide comprehensive maintenance services to ensure longevity and effectiveness:

  • Silicone Sealing: Ideal for flexible joints and areas exposed to high movement with excellent durability.
  • Polyurethane Sealing: Provides strong adhesion and is perfect for areas requiring durable, weather-resistant seals.
  • Acrylic Sealing: Suitable for areas that need to be painted over, offering a clean and professional finish.
  • Butyl Rubber Sealing: Great for sealing joints in roofing and guttering systems due to its excellent waterproofing properties.
