Our Services

Fire Protection Services & Methods

Our comprehensive fire protection services are designed to prevent fire spread and ensure safety. We specialize in various fire protection methods:

  • Fire Rated Sealing: Prevents the spread of fire and smoke through openings in walls, floors, and ceilings using high-quality materials and methods.
  • Fire Stopping: Enhances building fire resistance through installation of fire barriers, fire dampers, and fire-resistant coatings.
  • Penetration Seals: Installing seals around pipes, cables, and ducts to maintain fire resistance ratings.
  • Spray Applied Fireproofing: Using fire-resistant sprays for structural steel and other critical components.

Fire Caulking Types & Maintenance

We use various types of fire caulking to suit different applications and provide comprehensive maintenance services:

  • Intumescent Caulking: Expands when exposed to heat, sealing gaps and preventing fire spread with superior protection.
  • Silicone Fire Caulking: Provides flexibility and durability, suitable for a range of temperatures and applications.
  • Acrylic Fire Caulking: Easy to apply and paintable, ideal for interior fire protection applications.
  • Regular Maintenance: Comprehensive maintenance services including inspections, repairs, and upgrades to keep your fire protection systems optimal.
